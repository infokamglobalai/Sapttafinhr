# User Manual — Employee

## Login

1. Open your company link: `https://<company>.<domain>/auth/login/`
2. Enter email and password (HR sends credentials on joining)
3. Dashboard shows your leave balance, announcements, quick actions

## Attendance

- **Punch** — Attendance → Punch in / Punch out (same day)
- **My attendance** — calendar of present/absent/half-day
- **Regularization** — if you forgot to punch, submit a correction request with reason

## Leaves

1. **Leaves → Apply**
2. Choose leave type, dates, reason
3. Submit — goes to your manager (then HR if needed)
4. **My leaves** — track status; cancel if still pending
5. **Comp-off** — if your company uses it, request comp-off credit after working on off days; apply **CO** leave type when redeeming

## Payslips & tax

- **My payslips** — download PDF after HR publishes payroll
- **Tax declaration** — submit 80C/80D etc. before HR deadline
- **Form 16** — download after HR issues for the financial year

## Expenses

1. **My expenses → New claim**
2. Enter amount, category, date, upload receipt
3. Track approval status; payment appears in payroll when processed

## Loans

- **My loans** — view outstanding loans and EMI schedule (if HR assigned a loan)

## Performance

- **My reviews** — read manager feedback and acknowledge

## Policies & announcements

- Read **Announcements** on dashboard
- **Policies** — browse company policies; use **Ask policy** for AI Q&A (if enabled)

## HR AI chat

Click the chat icon. Examples:
- "What is my casual leave balance?"
- "List holidays in December"
- "Apply sick leave tomorrow"

The assistant only accesses **your** HR data in your company workspace.

## Notifications

Bell icon shows leave approvals, payslip published, Form 16, etc.

## Mobile

Open the same URL on your phone browser. **Add to Home Screen** for quick access (PWA).

## Need help?

Contact your HR admin — they can reset password, fix profile link, or adjust leave balance.

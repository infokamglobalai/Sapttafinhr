Why bundle still wins on profit

We discount ₹1,999 on the base, but Finance marginal cost is only ~₹950 — so Complete still earns ~₹2,100 more than HRMS-only at the same headcount, plus dual-product lock-in and lower churn.

Extra-employee economics

Every seat over 30 adds ₹111 revenue at ~₹30 cost — ~73% margin. Headcount growth is pure high-margin expansion MRR with no sales touch.

## Billing terms (attract 1-year, stay profitable)

| Term | Discount | 1–50 HRMS example |
|------|----------|-------------------|
| Monthly | 0% | ₹4,999/mo |
| 3 months | **3%** | ₹14,547 prepay → ₹4,849/mo effective |
| 6 months | **6%** | ₹28,194 prepay → ₹4,699/mo effective |
| **1 year** ⭐ | **12%** | **₹52,789/yr → ₹4,399/mo effective** |
| 2 years | 18% | ₹98,378 → ₹4,099/mo effective |

**Why 12% on 1 year (not 17%):**  
- Customer saves **₹7,199/year** — easy to show on website  
- You keep **88%** of annual revenue vs “2 months free” (83%)  
- Badge: **“Most popular — save 12%”**

### 1–50 Complete + 1 year

- Monthly: ₹6,999  
- Annual prepay: **₹73,909/yr** (12% off) → **₹6,159/mo effective**  
- vs separate annual: ~₹1,05,578 → save big on Complete

# User Manual — Manager

## Login

Use your company workspace URL and manager credentials. You need an **employee profile** linked to your user to see team data.

If you see "Manager profile not found," contact HR to link your login to your employee record.

## Dashboard

Shows your team snapshot: pending approvals, who's on leave, quick links.

## Leave approvals

1. **Leaves → Pending** (or notification bell)
2. Open request — see dates, type, balance
3. **Approve** or **Reject** with optional comment
4. HR can override if you're unavailable

## Attendance

- **Team attendance** — view direct reports' month calendar
- **Regularizations** — approve punch corrections for your team

## Expenses

- **Team expenses** — pending reimbursement claims from direct reports
- Approve or reject; approved claims flow to HR for payroll payment

## Performance reviews

1. **Performance → Team reviews**
2. For each direct report + open cycle, click **Create/Edit review**
3. Optional: use **AI draft** — paste bullet notes, get structured draft
4. Submit review; employee acknowledges when published

## Comp-off (if enabled)

Approve comp-off credit requests from team members who worked on holidays/weekends.

## What managers cannot do

- Add/edit employees
- Run payroll or download bank files
- Change leave types or holiday calendar
- Access other teams' data

## AI assistant

Managers can ask:
- "Who on my team is on leave next week?"
- "Show attendance summary for my team"

Data is limited to your direct reports.

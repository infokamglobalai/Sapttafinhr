How it works

  1. Set company name, logo, and address (letterhead settings) — or override per template.
  2. Choose India / GCC built-in layout, or upload custom HTML for your exact company format.
  3. Pick footer: system generated (no signature), certified (signatory), or custom text.
  4. Mark one template as Default — used when payroll is approved.

Custom HTML uses Jinja2 variables: employee, record, company, earnings_rows, net_payable_fmt, net_payable_words, logo_url.

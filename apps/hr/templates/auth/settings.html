How employee invites work

  1. You create an employee with an official email.
  2. HR generates a signed invite link (valid 7 days).
  3. If SMTP is live, the link is emailed automatically; otherwise you copy it from the credentials screen.
  4. Employee opens the link → sets their password → is logged in.
  5. They sign in again at {{ PLATFORM_BASE_URL }} → HR employee login.

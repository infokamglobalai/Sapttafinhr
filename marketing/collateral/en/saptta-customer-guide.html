4. Manager guide

  • My Team — see who is on leave, pending approvals.
  • Leave — approve or reject with optional comment; HR can override.
  • Regularizations — approve punch corrections for direct reports.
  • Expenses — approve team reimbursement claims → HR pays via payroll.
  • Performance — write/submit reviews for open cycles (optional AI draft).
  • Service requests — hardware/software requests route through you first.

5. Employee self-service

  • Attendance — punch in/out; request regularization if you missed a punch.
  • Leave — apply, track status, cancel if still pending; comp-off type CO if enabled.
  • Payslips — download PDF after HR publishes payroll.
  • Tax & Form 16 — submit declarations; download Form 16 when issued.
  • Expenses & loans — submit claims; view EMI schedule if assigned.
  • Help & requests — IT, HR, or hardware tickets with status tracking.
  • Policies — read company policies; ask AI for plain-language answers.

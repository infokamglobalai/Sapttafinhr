Important distinction

Two different “admins” — don’t mix them up

❌ NOT what you buy — Saptta platform team

  • Internal Saptta operators only
  • Console at /superadmin
  • Manages all customer companies
  • Billing ops, support, provisioning

✅ What YOU get — Workspace owner

  • The email that signed up & paid
  • Controls only your company
  • HR: Owner + full HR admin
  • Finance: OWNER (if licensed)
Sales line: “You are not getting Saptta’s internal superadmin. You get your own secure company workspace — like your own private HR & Finance tenant.”
